What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is the first decentralized digital currency, created in 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto. It's not controlled by any government or bank.

Think of it as digital gold: scarce, secure, and borderless.

2

The History

Launched during the 2008 financial crisis, Bitcoin was designed to be a trustless system. Its blockchain ensures transparency.
