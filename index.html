What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is the first decentralized digital currency, created in 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto. It's not controlled by any government or bank—it's peer-to-peer money for the internet age.

Think of it as digital gold: scarce, secure, and borderless. No middlemen, just you and your wallet.

2

The History of Bitcoin

Launched during the 2008 financial crisis, Bitcoin was designed to be a trustless system. Its blockchain technology ensures transparency and immutability.

From pizza purchases to billion-dollar markets, Bitcoin has evolved into a global phenomenon. 🚀

3

How to Get Started

1. Choose a Wallet: Secure your keys with apps like Electrum or hardware like Ledger.

2. Buy Bitcoin: Use exchanges like Coinbase or Binance. Start small!

3. Learn & Invest: Educate yourself—crypto is volatile but rewarding.

4

Security Tips

🔒 Never share your private keys. Use 2FA. Store offline for large amounts.

🛡️ Beware of scams—verify sources. DYOR (Do Your Own Research)!

Your crypto is only as secure as your habits.
